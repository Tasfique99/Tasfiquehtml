About My School

International Hope School Bangladesh (IHSB) is a private English-medium school located in Uttara, Dhaka. Established in 1996, it offers education from Playgroup to Grade 12 following the Cambridge curriculum.

The school hosts many exciting events like the Bangla Olympiad, STEM Fest, and Business Carnival that promote academics, culture, entrepreneurship, and sports.

Since 2018, IHSB became independent and is now managed by Starling Education, providing quality education to thousands of students across multiple campuses.
