Who am I?

My name is Tasfique. I am a passionate learner who enjoys discovering how technology works and how it can make our lives better. From websites to apps, I love building things that are useful and meaningful.

I believe in learning by doing. Whether it’s coding a new feature, fixing a bug, or trying out a new programming language, every step helps me grow as a developer.

Outside of coding, I enjoy reading tech blogs, participating in competitions, and sharing knowledge with others. I’m curious, motivated, and always up for a challenge!
